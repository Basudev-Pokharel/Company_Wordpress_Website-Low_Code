What Does a Website Cost in Finland

Website pricing depends on scope and features.

  • Basic website (3–5 pages): €800 – €2,000

  • Business website (custom design, CMS): €2,000 – €5,000

  • E-commerce website: €3,500 – €10,000+

Final price depends on design complexity, integrations, and functionality.

How Long Does Website Development Take?

  • Basic website: 2–3 weeks

  • Business website: 3–6 weeks

  • E-commerce website: 4–8 weeks

Timeline depends on project complexity and content readiness.
